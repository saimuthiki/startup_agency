Years of Legacy

Our Story

A Family Legacy on the Highway

What began in 2010 as a humble roadside eatery by the Naidu family has blossomed into one of the most beloved landmarks on the Chittoor-Bangalore Highway. Shri Venkata Naidu, a passionate home cook, envisioned a place where weary highway travellers could experience the warmth of a home-cooked Andhra meal—prepared with fresh ingredients, traditional recipes, and genuine care.

Today, Nandi Food Plaza stands as a testament to that dream. Spread across a sprawling property with a 200+ seat A/C dining hall, a fully equipped function hall for celebrations, ample parking, and modern amenities including an EV charging station, we have grown while staying true to our roots. Every dish that leaves our kitchen carries the same love and authenticity that our founder brought to his very first plate of Andhra meals.

With over 19,400 reviews and counting, we are honoured to be the trusted rest stop for thousands of families, bus travellers, and road-trippers making the journey between Chittoor and Bangalore.

Find Us

How to Reach Us

From Bangalore (120 km)

  1. Take NH44 towards Chittoor / Tirupati
  2. Continue on the Chittoor-Bangalore Highway (NH40)
  3. After crossing Mulbagal, continue towards Bangarupalem
  4. Nandi Food Plaza is on the left side at Nalagampalli, just before Bangarupalem
  5. Look for the large "Nandi Food Plaza" signboard

Approx. travel time: 2.5 hours

From Chittoor (50 km)

  1. Head towards Bangalore on the Chittoor-Bangalore Highway (NH40)
  2. Pass through Bangarupalem town
  3. Nandi Food Plaza is on the right side at Nalagampalli, just after Bangarupalem

Approx. travel time: 1 hour
