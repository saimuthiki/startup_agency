About Us

A Legacy of Authentic Andhra Flavours

Situated on the bustling Chittoor-Bangalore Highway near Bangarupalem, Nandi Food Plaza has become a beloved destination for travellers and locals alike. With over 19,400 Google reviews, we've earned our reputation through consistent quality and heartfelt hospitality.

Our air-conditioned restaurant serves a wide range of South Indian, Andhra, and vegetarian specialities prepared with fresh ingredients and traditional recipes. Whether you're stopping for a quick meal on the highway or celebrating a special occasion in our function hall, Nandi Food Plaza promises an experience you'll remember.
